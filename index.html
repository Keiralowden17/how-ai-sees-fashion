Platform scores

How scores are calculated
Emotional intelligence (30 pts)
Did the platform acknowledge the anxiety behind the question, not just the practical need?
Personalization (25 pts)
Did it ask who you are before answering, or make silent assumptions?
Specificity (25 pts)
Did it name real brands or give generic fabric advice?
Gender neutrality (10 pts)
Did it avoid defaulting to a gender, or at least offer both?
Format quality (10 pts)
Was the output easy to read and navigate?
Statistical intelligence
Linear regression trend lines, anomaly detection, and platform consistency. Computed locally — no API, no keys.
These stats need at least 3 audits across different dates. The more you log, the sharper the signal.

Platform consistency scores
High consistency means a platform behaves similarly regardless of what you ask. Low consistency means it is unpredictable — good on some prompts, not on others. Measured using coefficient of variation across scored audits.
